This is the darkest supper.

The first and last meal of a lifetime. One meal. World renowned chefs. Four guests. Gone forever.

The Darkest Supper is a once in a lifetime culinary experience for four exclusive guests, dining in an undisclosed location.

Gathering together a degustation menu prepared by world renowned chefs, those partaking at the Darkest Supper will feed on meals that have never been created before and will never taste again.

The four Darkest Supper guests, when chosen, will be taken to a secluded forest location. The guests will have their senses blocked until they arrive at their destination – an architecturally designed cabin made from sustainable materials.

There, they will surrender their electronic devices and sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement. They may never reveal the tastes and textures of the meal, the identities of their dining companions, or the recipes that lay before them.

After the conclusion of the meal, diners and chefs will partake in razing the cabin and all its contents to the ground – so Mother Earth can claim back what is rightfully hers.
